About The Journey of a Widow

We are a community-driven initiative committed to walking with widows through healing, empowerment, and hope.

  • Providing emotional support, counseling, and a safe space to grieve and grow.
  • Organizing empowerment programs and training to promote self-reliance.
  • Raising awareness and advocating for widows‚Äô rights, dignity, and inclusion in society.

Through workshops, events, and outreach, we strive to create a world where no widow walks alone. Our mission is rooted in compassion, strength, and the belief that healing begins with connection and community.

Our Partners in Hope

We are grateful to the organizations and individuals who walk this journey with us.

Support Groups

We offer emotional support circles and peer mentorship to help widows share, heal, and grow together.

Skills Training

Our vocational and entrepreneurship programs equip widows with skills to become self-reliant and financially empowered.

Advocacy & Awareness

We raise awareness about the rights and needs of widows, working to end stigma and discrimination in society.

Community Events

We organize regular events, workshops, and outreach missions to bring widows together and celebrate resilience.

Explore the Programs We Offer

Widow Empowerment Workshops

Interactive sessions focused on personal development, healing, and rebuilding self-confidence among widows.

Mental Health & Counseling

Access to group and individual therapy sessions with trained counselors to support emotional well-being.

Financial Literacy Training

Programs designed to help widows manage personal finances, savings, and business budgeting skills.

Entrepreneurship Support

Helping widows start or grow small businesses through mentorship, training, and startup kits.

Community Support Groups

Peer-led gatherings for shared experiences, encouragement, and empowerment through sisterhood.

Legal Awareness Seminars

Training and awareness on inheritance rights, property laws, and legal protection for widows.
